Texts are the most important element on your web. Is the only element that the user can read and understand.

Titles

By adding the class "mc-title", you will set the text as the main text.

  • <h1 class="mc-title">My Title</h1>

My Title

My Title




*The title will take different styles depending on the situation.


Subtitles

By adding the class "mc-subtitle", you will set the text as the secondary tittle.

  • <h2 class="mc-subtitle">My Subtitle</h2>

My Subtitle

My Subtitle




*The subtitle will take different styles depending on the situation.


Normal text

By adding the class "mc-text", you will set the text as normal text.

  • <p class="mc-text">My Text</p>

My Text

My Text


*The text will take different styles depending on the situation.


Legends and Notes

If you add the class "mc-note", you will set the text as small and italic text.

  • <p class="mc-note">My Note</p>

My Note


Others

We also provide you several classes to modify your text, such as "mc-justified", so your text will be like that: justified.

  • <p class="mc-text mc-justified">Here goes my justified text.</p>

  • mc-left
  • mc-justified
  • mc-center
  • mc-right

Another very cool thing are pills, so you can have some kind of indicator like Alert or Ok, or even Warning.

You can use mc-pill or mc-big-pill along with our background and color classes to customize your own pill.
