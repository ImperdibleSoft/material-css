Lists are used to enumerate things, so they are really useful for us.

Normal list

To start, normal list with the "mc-list" class. This will set the styles for your list, so it's going to look good.


  • <ul class="mc-list" >
    • <li></li>
    • ...
  • </ul>

List layout

List layout is an amazing modification of a normal list, where you can display things such as friends, albums, events, folders, etc.

Simply use the mc-layout="list" attribute on any item, and place the "mc-list-item" class for each list item you have. Automatically, you will have an amazing and beautiful list.


  • <ul mc-layout="list" >
    • <li class="mc-list-item"></li>
    • ...
  • </ul>

Into your list, you can write plain text, or you can use MC items to make them look really good. For example, you can use a normal "img", a "mc-title", and a "mc-text" or a "mc-note".


  • <ul mc-layout="list" >
    • <li class="mc-list-item">
      • <img src="" />
      • <h1 class="mc-title">My title</li>
      • <p class="mc-text | mc-note">My text</li>
    • </li>
  • </ul>

Autocomplete

The autocomplete is an very special item. It is a powerful tool to make quick actions, not just give some autocomplete suggestions. You can use it for display a menu with quick actions when entering a data (like a name, or an ID).

Just place an item with the "mc-autocomplete" class into your mc-input, next to your input and your mc-label. This will prepeare the container for the autocomplete menu.

Inside of it, you can place an item with the mc-layout="list", as we show previously, with all its "mc-list-item" into it, and you will have a perfect autocomplete menu.


  • <div class="mc-input" >
    • <input type="text" />
    • <label class="mc-label">Autocomplete label</label>
    • <div class="mc-autocomplete" >
      • <div mc-layout="list" >
        • <div class="mc-list-item" > <p class="mc-text" > Option 1 </p> </div>
        • <div class="mc-list-item" > <p class="mc-text" > Option 2 </p> </div>
      • </div>
    • </div>
  • </div>
