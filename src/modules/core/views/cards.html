Cards are a good way to present the content on your web. These cards help you to present the content in an ordered way, well distributed and with enought space to make them beautiful.

We know there are a lot of ways to present content, so we have prepeared a lot of different card types, so you can use the one you like.

Normal cards

To start, just set a div with a "mc-card" class. This will construct the main conintainer for the card.

Into this div, you can place a div with "mc-header", "mc-content" or a "mc-footer" classes. You can combine them as you prefer.


  • <div class="mc-card" >
    • <div class="mc-content" >
      • <h2 class="mc-subtitle" ></h2>
      • <p class="mc-text" ></p>
      • <p class="mc-text" ></p>
    • </div>
  • </div>

Cards with buttons

Sometimes, you need to interact with cards, and buttons are here for this! You can use buttons in the footer, and they will adapt automatically to this position, or you can use in the content, so the will look normal.

      • <h2 class="mc-subtitle" ></h2>
      • <p class="mc-text" ></p>
    • </div>

Cards with header images

It's time to start with layouts. Layouts are a great thing we've implemented to modify the appearance of some components. For example: cards.

The first layout we are going to use is "header-image". This will make the image placed on the header to fill all the available space, so we add the attribute mc-layout="header-image" to our card.


  • <div class="mc-card" mc-layout="header-image">
    • <div class="mc-header">
      • <img src=""/>
      • <h1 class="mc-title mc-color-white" ></h1>
    • </div>
    • <div class="mc-content">
      • <h1 class="mc-text" ></h1>
    • </div>
  • </div>


Card with layout

This card has the mc-layout implemented.

Cards with lateral images

Maybe you want another layouts, to make your site more dynamic. You also can use lateral images to occupy less space with images.

For this cases, you can use these mc-layouts: left-image and right-image.

Grid of images

In case you want to place more than 1 image, they'll take too much space, so we've prepeared the "grid-images" layout. This will reduce the image sizes so you can display as much as you want.

On this case, we added the attribute mc-layout="grid-images" to our card.


  • <div class="mc-card" mc-layout="grid-images" >
    • <div class="mc-header" >
      • <img src="" />
      • <h1 class="mc-title" ></h1>
      • <h2 class="mc-subtitle" ></h2>
    • </div>
    • <div class="mc-content" >
      • <p class="mc-text" ></p>

        • <img src="" />

        • <img src="" />
